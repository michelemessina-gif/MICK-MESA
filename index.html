Story

My Story

I was born in Sicily and moved to the UK at 19. London, Manchester, and Malta became part of my journey, shaping the music I write today.

Mick Mesa is the beginning of a bigger artistic vision: songs written from survival, emotional truth, and the desire to create something honest from everything I have lived.
